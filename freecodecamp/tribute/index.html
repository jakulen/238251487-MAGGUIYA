Dr. Norman Borlaug

Dr. Norman Borlaug, Nobel Peace Prize Laureate, known for his work in agricultural science and world hunger alleviation.
Dr. Norman Borlaug was an American agronomist and humanitarian. Often referred to as the "Father of the Green Revolution," he helped save over a billion people from starvation through his pioneering work in plant genetics and crop production. He was awarded the Nobel Peace Prize in 1970 for his efforts to combat hunger worldwide.
